How to scan dense 16-digit barcodes:
  • Ensure bright, even lighting on the barcode
  • Hold device steady about 6-12 inches from the barcode
  • Try tilting the device at a slight angle (15-30 degrees)
  • Use the zoom buttons if the barcode appears too small
  • If scanning fails, use the manual entry option below
